# Getting Started with Cursive

Welcome to Cursive! This guide will help you get started with our platform and start turning your website visitors into booked meetings.

## What is Cursive?

Cursive is a lead marketplace platform that helps you identify anonymous website visitors and convert them into qualified leads. Our platform integrates with AudienceLab to track visitor behavior and enrich lead data.

## Quick Setup

Follow these steps to get started:

### 1. Sign Up

Create your account at [leads.meetcursive.com](https://leads.meetcursive.com). You can sign up using:

- Google OAuth (recommended for fastest setup)
- Email and password

### 2. Set Your Preferences

Navigate to **Settings â†’ Lead Preferences** and configure:

- **Industries**: Select the industries you want to target
- **Locations**: Choose states, cities, or ZIP codes
- **Lead Cap**: Set your maximum monthly lead limit

### 3. Add Credits

Go to **Billing** to purchase lead credits. We offer several packages:

- **Starter**: 100 credits
- **Growth**: 500 credits
- **Professional**: 1,000 credits
- **Enterprise**: Custom pricing

**New users get 100 free credits to get started!**

### 4. Integrate Your Tools

Connect your favorite tools in **Integrations**:

- **Slack**: Get real-time notifications
- **Zapier**: Connect to 1000+ apps
- **Custom Webhooks**: Build your own integration

## Viewing Your Leads

Once you're set up, leads will start appearing in **My Leads**. Each lead includes:

- Full name and contact information
- Company details
- Industry and location
- Lead score (deliverability rating)

You can manage leads by:

- Accepting or rejecting them
- Exporting to CSV
- Viewing detailed profiles
