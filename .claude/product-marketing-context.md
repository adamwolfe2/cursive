## What We Do

Cursive solves the problem that 98% of website visitors leave without filling out a form. We:

1. **Identify** anonymous website visitors (70% identification rate)
2. **Enrich** them with verified contact data from 220M+ consumer and 140M+ business profiles
3. **Activate** through AI-powered multi-channel outreach (email, LinkedIn, SMS)
4. **Convert** prospects into booked meetings automatically

### Key Differentiation
- **Real-time identification** (not batch processing)
- **Activation included** (not just data delivery)
- **70% ID rate** (industry-leading)
- **AI SDR built-in** (autonomous outreach + follow-ups)
- **Unified B2B + B2C data** (220M consumer + 140M business profiles)
