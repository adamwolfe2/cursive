## Framework Implementation Summary

### Seven Sweeps Copy Editing Applied to All Posts

1. **Clarity Sweep**: Removed jargon, simplified complex concepts, added concrete examples
2. **Voice & Tone Sweep**: Professional yet conversational, data-driven but accessible
3. **"So What" Sweep**: Every feature connected to business outcomes
4. **Proof Sweep**: Cursive stats integrated (70%, 220M+, 140M+, 450B+, 200+)
5. **Scan Sweep**: H2 every 300-400 words, bullets, tables, bold key points
6. **CTA Sweep**: Specific CTAs matching content (not generic "learn more")
7. **One More Time Sweep**: Flow verified, links checked, brand voice consistent

### SEO Optimization

**Title Structure**: All titles follow pattern "How to/Guide to [Primary Keyword] [Year]"
- Average title length: 55-65 characters
- Keywords front-loaded
- Year included for freshness (2026)

**Meta Descriptions**:
- Length: 150-160 characters
- Primary keyword + value prop + CTA
- All unique and compelling

**URL Slugs**:
- Format: `/blog/[category]/[keyword-rich-slug]/`
- Under 60 characters
- Hyphenated, not underscored

**Keyword Integration**:
- Primary keyword in title, first 100 words, 2+ H2s, conclusion, meta
- Secondary keywords in H2/H3s and body
- Natural density (1-2%)
- Internal link anchor text keyword-rich

### AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)

**FAQ Sections**: Every post includes 8 FAQs
- Question-based headings
- 100-150 word direct answers
- Natural, conversational language
- Structured for featured snippets

**Question-Based H2s**:
- "What is..." "How does..." "Why..." "When should..."
- Answers in first 1-2 sentences after heading
- Self-contained sections

**Structured Content**:
- Tables for comparisons
- Bullets for lists
- Bold for definitions
- Short paragraphs (2-4 sentences)

### Internal Linking Strategy

**Every Post Includes**:
- 1 solution page link (visitor-identification, direct-mail, audience-builder, intent-audiences)
- 2-3 related blog post links
- 1 bottom-funnel link (platform, pricing, demo)
- 1 integrations page link
- Average 5-7 internal links per post

**Link Placement**:
- First link within 500 words
- 3-4 links throughout body
- 1-2 links in conclusion
- Natural anchor text (not "click here")

### Product Mentions

**Frequency**: 3-5 Cursive mentions per post
**Types**:
- Subtle (2-3 per post): "With tools like Cursive, you can..."
