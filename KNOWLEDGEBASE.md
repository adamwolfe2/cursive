## HOW THE DATA WORKS (technical depth for sophisticated buyers)

### Identity Resolution Stack

**Layer 1: Device Fingerprinting + IP Intelligence**
- We don't rely on cookies (cookie-free identification)
- Device fingerprinting creates a persistent identifier across sessions
- IP intelligence maps to household/business level
- Works even with VPNs and incognito (at reduced rates)

**Layer 2: SuperPixel Technology**
- JavaScript tag installed on client's website (5 min setup)
- Captures: page views, time on page, scroll depth, form interactions, return visits
- Matches anonymous visitors against the 280M identity graph in real-time
- Returns: name, email, phone, company, title, LinkedIn, social profiles
- NOT batch processing — real-time matching as visitors hit the site

**Layer 3: Online-to-Offline (O2O) Matching**
- Connects individual online behavior to offline business intent
- B2B2C linkage: we know when a person at Company X is researching Topic Y
- This is the key differentiator vs. cookie-based solutions

**Layer 4: UID2 / Universal ID Framework**
- Compatible with The Trade Desk's UID2 framework
- Deterministic matching (not probabilistic)
- Privacy-preserving: hashed email-based IDs
- Enables cross-platform activation without third-party cookies
- Future-proof against cookie deprecation (Chrome, Safari, Firefox)

### Why multiple domains/inboxes?
- Distributes sending volume across domains to protect reputation
- Each inbox sends ~25-30 emails/day (industry best practice)
- Domain warmup takes 2-3 weeks before full volume
- If one domain has issues, others keep sending
- Separate domains protect client's main domain reputation

### Copy Generation Engine
- Two-call AI pattern: Angle Selection then Copy Writing
- 3 different messaging angles per campaign (tests different approaches)
- Spintax for A/B testing (automatic subject line and opening variants)
- 95-word max per email (deliverability optimized)
- No spam trigger words, no excessive links
- Quality check system catches issues before sending
- Auto-fix loop corrects any quality failures
